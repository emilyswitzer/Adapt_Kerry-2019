Court Accompaniment Service

The Courts can provide protection for victims of Domestic Violence. However, we understand that the Court System can be daunting for anyone, but we can help you through our Court Accompaniment Service. This service (funded by the Department of Justice through the Commission for the Support of Victims of Crime) is free of charge and available to any woman who is affected by Domestic Violence and attending Family Law Court Hearings in the District, Circuit or High Courts sitting in County Kerry.

Going to Court can be a very difficult and stressful experience for women who have experienced domestic abuse. As part of the service we provide, we can accompany you through the legal system regarding domestic abuse. Staff have a knowledge and understanding of the family law system and a working knowledge of the legal orders that may be available to you.

Some of the reasons for attending court can be:

  • Applications and Hearings for protection, safety and barring orders
  • Custody, Access
  • Maintenance Orders
  • Breaches of Domestic Abuse Orders
  • Separation and Divorce Hearings

We can provide you with support through what can be a difficult process through:

  • Information on the legal options that may be open to you
  • A referral to the Legal Aid Board or Family Law Solicitor
  • The completion of a Legal Aid Application Form
  • Accompaniment to your appointment with your solicitor
  • Accompaniment to the Court Clerk's Office to complete paperwork for your Hearing
  • Accompaniment to your Court Hearing(s)
  • Follow-up support following your Court Hearing(s)

Please feel free to contact us for more information on this service.

Our Outreach Support Workers are in attendance at the following Court venues in County Kerry:

  • Tralee
  • Listowel
  • Killarney
  • Killorglan
  • Dingle
  • Kenmare
  • Cahersiveen

We are very aware, that it may be many times that you need the support and services offered by the Refuge. Our service is always available to you, and we are here to support you in any way we can, and that you are welcome to come back to us if you ever need our service again in the future. Our Outreach Support service will be made available to you after you have moved on from the Refuge and can be accessed any time that suits you. Outreach will continue to support you around your emotional needs as well as any further support around accessing other supports; accompaniment or information around domestic abuse.

Please feel free to contact us for more information on this service.
