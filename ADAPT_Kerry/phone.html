Telephone Support Service

There is always someone here to listen to you if you want to talk. Our lines are open from 9am−12am, Monday−Friday & 1pm−11pm Saturday & Sunday.

Our telephone support service will offer you a confidential space to access support and information around your situation. We also support family members, friends and professionals who have concerns about a person, who might be experience domestic abuse.

We hope to empower women to better understand what may be happening for them and offer information and advocacy to gain a better understanding as to the options that may be available to them.

Our role is to believe and offer support around your situation, we understand the dynamics and impact of abuse and will not place blame on you in your situation.

We appreciate how difficult it can be to pick up the phone and tell someone about what is happening. We will give you time and support during the phone call and respect your judgements on what you are comfortable in sharing. Talking about your experiences with us, may give you the opportunity to share with someone the difficulties and hardships faced in an abusive relationship.

Your safety is our main concern. We believe that you are the best judge of your own safety; we are here to talk you through the different options available. We are here to support you, not to judge but to listen and talk you through your options.
